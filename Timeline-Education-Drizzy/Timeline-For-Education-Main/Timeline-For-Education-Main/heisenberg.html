Heisenberg's Matrix Mechanics

Overview

In 1925, Werner Heisenberg developed matrix mechanics, the first complete formulation of quantum mechanics. This approach represented physical observables using matrices and introduced non-commutative algebra, a key aspect of quantum theory.
