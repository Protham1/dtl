Einstein's Photoelectric Effect

Overview

In 1905, Albert Einstein published his paper explaining the photoelectric effect, providing evidence for the particle-like behavior of light. This work was pivotal in establishing the concept of photons and earned Einstein the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1921.
